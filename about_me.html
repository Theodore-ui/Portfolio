Why I want a career in technology

I believe that technology is what defines us as a species. Ultimately, the difference between what civilisation was, is now and will be in the future is the capacity to produce, deploy and regulate technology. I want to be a part of the driving force behind this assessment, to push the boundaries of what is possible and what we can achieve as a species.

Global development is increasing at an exponential rate amid globalization. The relationship between global development and time shows we have passed the inflection point where 1 unit of time corresponds to a greater than 1 unit of global development. As we become more efficient in a globalised world the consumption of energy will increase and thus software to manage efficiency is fundamental. This is no more pressing than in the transportation industry. A career in technology would allow me to be apart of this. My logical thinking and problem-solving skills position me as a good candidate to create digital systems that are more efficient and effective than those we have currently.
